Privacy Policy

Introduction

MediScan values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

Data Collection

We collect images uploaded or captured through our platform solely for the purpose of analyzing potential vitamin deficiencies. No personally identifiable information is required to use our service, and images are stored temporarily for processing and then deleted.

Data Usage

The images you provide are processed using our AI models to generate analysis results. We do not share, sell, or disclose your images or results to third parties, except as required by law.

Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of any data we may have collected. Since we do not store images long-term, no additional action is typically required after analysis is complete.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
