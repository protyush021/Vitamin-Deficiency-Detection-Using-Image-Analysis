About MediScan

MediScan is an innovative tool designed to detect potential vitamin deficiencies using advanced image analysis technology. Developed as a project by two 4th-year Computer Science Engineering students at SRM Institute of Technology, MediScan leverages deep learning models to analyze images images of body parts such as eyes, face, lips, nails, and tongue to identify signs of vitamin deficiencies.

Our mission is to provide an accessible, user-friendly platform that empowers individuals to monitor their health and take proactive steps toward better nutrition and wellness. While MediScan is not a substitute for professional medical advice, it serves as a valuable educational tool to raise awareness about the importance of vitamins in maintaining overall health.
